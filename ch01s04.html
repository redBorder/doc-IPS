1.4. Obtaining the ISO

Redborder Enterprise Edition IPS is distributed as an ISO file that can be obtained in different ways. If you don’t have it, please contact our sales team (sales@redborder.com) or our support team (support@redborder.com) to provide you with the last available ISO file or a way to download it.
